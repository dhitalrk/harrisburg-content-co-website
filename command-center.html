Monthly Client Workflow — Step by Step

  • 1
    Client signs + pays — Send welcome email within 24 hours with the intake form link.
  • 2
    Receive intake form — Save as client profile. Note brand tone, key products, upcoming events, content preferences.
  • 3
    Request photos — Message client via their preferred channel. Tell them exactly what photos you need.
  • 4
    Build content package — Generate full monthly package: content calendar, all captions, hashtags, and graphics. Target turnaround: 2–3 days.
  • 5
    Send preview for approval — Email or message client with full content package. Give them 48 hours to review and request changes.
  • 6
    Make revisions if needed — Usually 0–2 small changes. Turnaround: same day or next day.
  • 7
    Schedule all posts — Log into Meta Business Suite and schedule all posts at the times shown in the calendar. Confirm with client once done.
  • 8
    Mid-month check-in — Around the 15th, send a 3-line update: top performing post, total engagement, what's working. Takes 5 minutes.
  • 9
    Next month package sent before month-end — Client should receive Month 2 content before Month 1 ends. No gaps. This drives renewals.
  • 10
    Renewal is automatic — If they're on Stripe subscription, it charges automatically. Keep delivering and they'll stay.
💡 Pro tip: Build a Google Drive folder for each client — one folder per month with their photos, final content package, and approved calendar. Keeps everything clean and makes it easy to reference past content so you never repeat a post.

📦 Tax Forms You'll Use

1099-K: From Stripe — your income statement. Mailed/emailed by Jan 31.
Schedule C: Profit & loss from your business. Attached to your 1040.
Schedule SE: Self-employment tax calculation (15.3%).
Form 1040-ES: Used to calculate and pay quarterly estimates.
PA-40: Pennsylvania state income tax return.

💡 Quick Tips
