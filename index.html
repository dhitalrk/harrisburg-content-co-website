FAQs

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How does payment work? +
All payments are processed securely through Stripe — the same payment platform used by Amazon and Shopify. You can pay with any major credit or debit card. Your card is charged monthly on the same date you signed up. You'll receive a receipt by email for every charge.
Is my payment information secure? +
Yes — we never see or store your card details. All payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe, which is PCI DSS Level 1 certified (the highest level of payment security). Your card information goes directly to Stripe's servers, never ours.
Do I need to sign a long-term contract? +
No contracts at all. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We earn your business every month by delivering results.
What do I need to provide each month? +
Just a few phone photos of your products, space, or team — and a quick heads-up on any upcoming specials or events. That's it. We write, design, and schedule everything else.
How fast will I see results? +
Most clients see improved engagement within the first 30 days. Real business results — calls, bookings, walk-ins from social — typically show within 60–90 days of consistent posting.
Can I approve content before it goes live? +
Always. We send you the full month's package for review before scheduling anything. You have 48 hours to request changes — we make them the same day.
What if I want to cancel? +
Just email or text us 30 days before your next billing date. No fees, no penalties. Your subscription ends at the close of your current paid period and you keep all content we've already delivered.
